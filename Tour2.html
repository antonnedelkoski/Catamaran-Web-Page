Some facts about the destination

This grand historic Orthodox church has the best views in Ohrid, perched high up on the cliff overlooking Lake Ohrid. It was built during the 13th century and located just above Kaneo beach, Ohrid. Also know as Church of St John of Kaneo. A great spot to capture special moments and a famously perfect spot for a beautiful sunrises and sunsets.
